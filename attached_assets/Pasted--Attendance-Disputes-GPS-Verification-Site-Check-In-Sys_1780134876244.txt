# Attendance Disputes, GPS Verification & Site Check-In System

## Purpose

Attendance is a core feature of OnSite.

Attendance records directly impact:

* Reliability Scores
* Punctuality Scores
* Worker Reputation
* Payroll Approval
* Invoicing
* Future Job Opportunities

For this reason, attendance records must be accurate, auditable, and challengeable.

---

# Attendance Workflow

Each working day, the Company/Site Manager must record attendance for every worker.

Available attendance statuses:

* On Time
* Late
* No Show
* Not Required Today
* Site Cancelled

Attendance submissions should be quick and mobile-friendly.

Target completion time:

Under 30 seconds for an entire workforce.

---

# Worker Attendance Review

Workers should be able to view all attendance records.

For every attendance record, workers can:

* Accept Attendance Record
* Raise Dispute

---

# Attendance Dispute Creation

When a worker raises a dispute, they must choose a reason.

Reasons:

* Incorrect No Show
* Incorrect Late Mark
* Incorrect Hours Worked
* Wrong Date
* Site Cancellation
* Incorrect Site Assignment
* Other

# Site Geofence

Each job site has:

* Site Address
* Exact Site Pin

The company must pin the exact site entrance location.

Examples:

* Main Gate
* Welfare Cabin
* Logistics Entrance
* Site Office

---

## Geofence Radius

Default:

100 metres

Admin configurable.

---

# Arrival Detection

When a worker enters the site geofence:

Trigger:

"You appear to have arrived at site."

Options:

* Check In
* Dismiss

---

# GPS Attendance Record

Store:

* Timestamp
* Latitude
* Longitude
* Distance from Site Pin

This creates supporting attendance evidence.

---

# GPS Evidence During Disputes

When a dispute is raised, the system automatically checks:

Was the worker inside the site geofence during the disputed period?

Examples:

### Yes

Worker was detected:

07:12 AM

Distance:

23 metres from Site Pin

Evidence Status:

Strong Supporting Evidence

---

### No

No GPS activity recorded near site.

Evidence Status:

No GPS Support

---

# Attendance Under Review

If a dispute is raised:

Attendance Status becomes:

Attendance Under Review

During review:

* Reliability Score is frozen
* Punctuality Score is frozen
* Payroll is placed on hold
* Invoice generation is placed on hold

No penalties should be applied until the dispute is resolved.

# Smart Attendance Validation

When a site manager marks:

"No Show"

The system should automatically check:

1. Did worker enter site geofence?
2. Did worker check in?
3. Was GPS recorded near site?
4. Did worker previously attend that project?

If GPS evidence exists:

Flag:

"Potential Attendance Conflict Detected"

Require confirmation before finalising the No Show.

This reduces false no-shows and protects worker reliability.

---

# Long-Term Objective

Create the most trusted attendance and workforce verification system in construction.

The platform should know:

* Who attended
* When they arrived
* How reliable they are
* Which projects they worked on
* Whether attendance records are supported by evidence

This data becomes significantly more valuable than traditional CVs and allows OnSite to automate recruitment, payroll, compliance, workforce management, and worker reputation tracking.
